Our Hiring Process

Step 1

Resume Review

We carefully evaluate each candidate's resume to ensure their experience and qualifications align with the roles we fill.

Step 2

English & Communication Screening

We assess fluency, grammar, and accent to ensure candidates can communicate effectively in professional settings.

Step 3

Skill Assessment

Candidates undergo testing tailored to their area of expertise, evaluating both technical and soft skills to confirm their proficiency.

Step 4

Internal Interview

Our team conducts in-depth interviews to assess candidates’ professionalism, problem-solving abilities, and cultural fit.

Step 5

Technology Check

We verify that each candidate has reliable technology and a stable internet connection, ensuring they’re ready to work remotely without disruptions.

Step 6

Specialized Evaluation

For roles requiring specific expertise, we conduct additional evaluations to validate their technical abilities and performance under role-specific tasks.
